#Transforming Healthcare Performance using AI Analytics

#Overview

This MBA dissertation explores how AI analytics can improve healthcare performance through early diagnosis, predictive insights, and operational efficiency in modern healthcare systems.

## Research Objectives
- Analyze the role of AI in healthcare analytics
- Study operational efficiency improvements through AI
- Understand predictive diagnosis applications
- Explore the business impact of AI-driven healthcare systems

#Tools & Technologies Used
- Python
- Tableau / Power BI
- Excel
- Statistical Analysis
- Data Visualization
- Predictive Analytics

#Key Insights
- AI improves early disease detection capabilities
- Predictive analytics supports faster decision-making
- Operational efficiency reduces delays and improves workflow
- Data-driven healthcare systems improve patient outcomes
